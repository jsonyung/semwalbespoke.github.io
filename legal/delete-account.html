Delete Your Semwal Bespoke Account
Last updated: November 14, 2025


How to delete your account
You can permanently delete your Semwal Bespoke account anytime from the app:
  1. Open Semwal Bespoke
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account
  3. Follow the confirmation steps
Once confirmed, your account will start the deletion process.

If you can't access the app
If you're unable to open the app (broken phone, login issues, etc.), you can request deletion manually.

Email us at:
support@semwalbespoke.in
Use the subject: Account Deletion Request

What we delete
When your account is deleted, the following will be permanently removed:
  • Your profile details
  • Measurements
  • Order history
  • Saved addresses
  • Wishlist & preferences
Non-personal, anonymous analytics data may be retained for internal reporting only.

How long deletion takes
  • Account deletion: Within 24–48 hours
  • Backup removal: Up to 30 days
  • Certain records may be kept if required under Indian law (e.g., invoices)
